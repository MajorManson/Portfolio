Volunteer

Global Perpective

Volunteer at a local school group called Global Perspective. The group was separated into two years. The first year will help in the local community and assist the second years with running the school fundraising event called Global Gala, to collect funds to acquire building materials, lodging, and food in a developing country for the second years to travel to. The second years will help the first years in their community activities and do the bulwork of the planning of the fundraising event. During my second year, we pulled off the most successful Gala in terms of raising funds, as well as travelling to Kochi, India to refurbish a local elementary school.
